Snail

Caracol

Snails symbolize life's progress, patience, and prudence. This alebrije is best for someone who is facing a time of change that wants to be able to rely on themselves if they need help. Being versatile ties into their prudence since being cautious about the decisions and actions one takes is what dictate what comes next. It takes a lot of preparation and wisdom for someone to not depend on anyone but themselves yet the snail manages to push through until they can't take it. Patience is also a characteristic many people lack but in the end, patience is a virtue.
