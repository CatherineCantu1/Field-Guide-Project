Dog

Perro

Dogs stand for happiness and loyalty. Like the saying goes “A dog is a man's best friend.” Best friends offer company, comfort, happiness, and unconditional loyalty. This alebrije has the tendency to uplift the spirits of others in a way other animals can't. With a high emotional intelligence, this alebrije could help those who have trouble being optimistic. As one of the most human-like animals, it's easy to consider them a part of a family and as someone who can be trusted. Being empathic is something many people strive for and with the support of this companion it's much easier.
