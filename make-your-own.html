Make Your Own!

  1. Find cardboard or some form of thicker paper. Fold that paper in half.
  2. Draw a sideways silhouette of the way you want your alebrije's body to look like.
  3. Cut around the silhouette. This will be the middle back part of the sculpture.
  4. Make a paste out of newspaper and glue combined. Mix the two until it looks sturdy but not too hard or too liquid.
  5. Use the paste to fill in the body and sculpt all the body parts. Do this in layers and allow them to fully dry otherwise your alebrije will begin to grow mold.
  6. Do one last layer with thinker paper and cover the entire sculpture.
  7. Finally, use vibrant acrylic paint to decorate the entire body with eye-catching patterns.

There are other methods out there but this is the most beginner friendly approach to take.
