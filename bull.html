Bull

Toro

Bull alebrijes exhibit power, hard work, and physical strength. Although these creatures can't literally make someone physically stronger, they give them the motivation to start whatever physical journey a person has been contemplating or make changes. Power comes in the form of having a bold personality. Whether it's for better or for worse for those around them, they work hard so it makes others want to work just as hard to prove themselves to be high-achievers. Even though the bull and dragon alebrijes are similar, the difference is that bulls are fine with being a part of a larger group.
