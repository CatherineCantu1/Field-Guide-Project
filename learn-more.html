Alebrijes have now become a statement piece in many ofrendas for DÃ­a De Los Muertos. As they have become engraved in Mexican art history, it's a simple yet symbolic way of including those who have passed on with modern culture. Although not originally planned by Linares, people have spread around the idea that these creatures are able to protect the ofrenda that they created, which helps the deceased reconnect with the people on the other side. This might have been further popularized by the Disney movie 'Coco', which features multiple alebrijes and the status they uphold for the people they were assigned to on the ofrenda. All things considered these creatures have forever changed Mexico with their unique look and initial inspiration.
