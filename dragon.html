Dragon

Dragón

Dragons mean having vitality, passion and strength. This alebrije is just right for those in positions of power or leaders within the spaces they reside in such as a class leader, a boss, or the head of the home. Dragons are known to be difficult challenges to pass through in folklore and religious stories so it makes sense that these attributes are attached to its character. These alebrijes offer the emotional and mental strength to take on whoever or whatever is trying to hurt them, therefore have a lot of passion towards what they do making them fit to lead.
