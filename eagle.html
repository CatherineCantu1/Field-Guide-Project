Eagle

Águila

Eagle alebrijes mean being noble and brave. As seen in the Mexican flag, Mexico is very culturally connected to eagles since the Aztec times. Eagles are believed to symbolize rebirth. At the end of the day, we're all human so having an admirable character is a hard thing to keep up all of the time. Being prideful of the work put into improving your personality takes bravery since it brings a lot of changes. If someone is in need of any bravery or the feeling of being high class, this is the alebrije for them.
