What are Alebrijes?

Originally from Oaxaca, Mexico, alebrijes are hand-crafted, detailed, figurines of animals with a dream-like twist. Though no two are exactly alike, all alebrijes remain consistent with their vibrant color markings. They feature detailed patterns of stripes, dots, geometric shapes, flowers, and flames. Many have bodies with inflated, twisted contortions. Their faces exhibit expressions ranging from tranquil and playful, to skeptical and sinister. Because of this, they can be found all the way from local markets to art museums around the world. They also make amazing gifts for loved ones.
